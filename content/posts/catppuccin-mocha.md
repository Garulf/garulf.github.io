---
title: Why I theme everything with Catppuccin Mocha
date: "2025-11-27"
tags: [design, dotfiles]
readTime: "4 min read"
---

Catppuccin Mocha is a warm dark palette with enough contrast for all-day use without the harshness of pure black themes. The four flavors — Latte, Frappé, Macchiato, Mocha — share the same accent hues, so switching between light and dark preserves the same visual identity across every surface.

I theme my terminal, editor, browser, and system with it because visual coherence reduces cognitive load. When every surface shares the same rose, mauve, and sapphire, nothing feels jarring. The JetBrains Mono pairing adds just enough technical personality without being gimmicky.
